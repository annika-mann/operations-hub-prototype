Customer Management

Search customers, products and invoices in one central place.

Example search queries
  • CUST-10482 → finds a customer by Customer ID
  • Example GmbH → shows all relevant entries for the company
  • PRD-051643 → finds the corresponding product or project
  • INV-2026-00482 → shows the corresponding invoice or credit note
  • example.test → shows matching domains, email addresses, etc.
  • First name Last name → shows matching contact persons

Search results are shown in three tables: Customers, products and invoices. Within the tables, active customer IDs are prioritized and shown first.

Clicking a Customer ID opens the Customer Profile. There you can find basic data, contacts, escalation priorities and relevant system handoffs.

The telephone icon can be used to check the customer’s telephone PIN.
