Area Overview

Customer Management handover prototype

This portfolio mock-up consolidates the initial Customer Management handover into one coherent area. It focuses on replacing a legacy customer information tool with read-only internal access to customer-related context.
Design Focus
Legacy replacementCustomer information tool
ScopeRead-only context
Entry modelFeature-level search
ExceptionTicket creation
Portfolio framing: This stage shows the initial feature-first handover logic before the later Customer Management main search and broader customer-context navigation emerged during MVP refinement.

Area building blocks

CP

Customer Profile

Customer master data, address and internal responsibilities in a read-only profile view.
CO

Contact Persons

Contacts, escalation priority and main contact information for a selected Customer ID.
US

Users

Cloud portal users and their assigned Customer IDs, roles and account states.
TS

Tickets

Support tickets, preview and ticket creation as the only initial active workflow.
OF

Offers

Read-only offer and deal overview for the selected customer context.
OR

Orders

Read-only internal order list with status and fulfillment context.
CT

Contracts

Read-only contract context with table, detail cards and basic access data.
IN

Invoices

Billing context with invoices, attachments, payment status and PDF actions.
Wider MVP context: Support and Reports were planned as adjacent Operations Hub areas. They are visible in the shell, but intentionally not expanded in this Customer Management handover prototype.
